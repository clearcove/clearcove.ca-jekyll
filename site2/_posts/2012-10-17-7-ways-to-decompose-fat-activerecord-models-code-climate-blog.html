This is a great article about decomposing "Fat Models". It advises against extracting methods into mixins and instead proposes the following refactorings:

  • Value Objects
  • Services Objects
  • Form Objects (using Virtus)
  • Query Objects
  • View Objects
  • Policy Objects
  • Decorators
