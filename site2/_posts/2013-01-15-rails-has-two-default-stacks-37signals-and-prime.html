Where 37Signals is:

  • ERB for view templates
  • MySQL for databases
  • MiniTest for testing
  • Fat Models, Skinny Controllers

And Prime is:

  • Haml for view templates
  • PostgreSQL for databases
  • Rspec/Cucumber for testing
  • Skinny models, controllers, and a service layer
